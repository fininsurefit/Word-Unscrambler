📚 What is the Gmail DOT Trick?

Gmail ignores dots (.) in the username part of email addresses. This means you can add or remove dots anywhere in your Gmail address, and emails will still be delivered to your inbox.

Example: If your email is johndoe@gmail.com, you can also use:

  • john.doe@gmail.com
  • j.ohndoe@gmail.com
  • j.o.h.n.d.o.e@gmail.com

All of these go to the same inbox!
